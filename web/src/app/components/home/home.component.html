Secure chat on your computer or smartphone

Share the room and password with your friends and you'll be able to chat securely with no need to install anything

1. Enter your nickname (lowercase) 2. Enter your room (lowercase) 3. Enter your room password


All the messages use end-to-end encryption, which means that only people who can read the messages are the ones communicating. No one else can see the conversation, not even a people with access to the safer.chat servers.
